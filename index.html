  Rellenong Hipon Recipe

Rellenong Hipon Ingridients
*100 Grams ground pork
*1/4 cup spring onions chopped
*1/4 cup singkamas, chopped
*1/4 cup carrots, shredded
*Canola oil
*2 cloves garlic, peeled and minced
*1 pc J shrimp Cube, mashed
*1/4 teaspoon pepper
*1/2kg large shrimp, peeled w/ tail on, deveined and butterflied
15 pieces spring roll wrappers, cut diagonally into two triangles

  • Step 1: Combine ground pork, spring onions, singkamas, carrots, garlic, mashed J Shrimp Cubes and pepper in a bowl. Mixed until well distributed and cube has dissolved.

  • Step 2: Place 1/2 teaspoon meat filling in the center of butterflied shrimp and press shrimp meat, to close around filling.

  • Step 3: Place stuffed shrimp on wrapper. Fold one corner of wrapper over shrimp, fold the corner at the tip, and then fold the opposite corner down. Seal by moistening sides with water and press lightly

  • Step 4: Heat oil in pan over medium heat. Oil should cover the stuffed shrimp to make cooking even. Fry until golden brown. Drain oil and serve with sweet chili sauce.

      Sinigang na Hipon Recipe

    Sinigang na Hipon Ingredients
    *500 grams shrimp, cleaned and de-veined
    *1 pack J Sinigang sa Sampalok Mix 44g
    *1 bunch kangkong
    *15 pcs sitaw, sliced into 2-inch pcs
    *8 pcs okra
    *1 pc eggplant, sliced
    *1 cup radish, sliced
    *1 pc tomato, wedged
    *3 pcs green pepper (siling pansigang)
    *1 pc red onion
    *8 cups water
    *fish sauce to taste

  • Step 1: Boil water in a cooking pot. Add onion, tomato, and radish. Cover and continue to boil for 8 minutes.

  • Step 2: Add shrimp. Cook for 1 minute.

  • Step 3:Add Knorr Sinigang sa Sampalok Mix. Stir until it dilutes completely. Cover and cook for 3 minutes.

  • Step 4:Add long green pepper, snake beans, okra, and eggplant. Stir. Cook for 5 minutes.

  • Step 5:Season with fish salt and ground black pepper.

  • Step 6:Add kangkong leaves. Turn off heat. Let it stay covered for 1 minute.

  • Step 7:Transfer to a serving bowl. Serve warm with rice.

      Shrimp Sinigang Recipe

    Shrimp Sinigang
    *4 cups rice washing
    *40 g. onions, quartered
    *100 g. tomatoes, quartered
    *500 g. fresh medium shrimps, with head, shell and tail intact
    *12 g. long green chili, slitted
    *50 g. radish, peeled and sliced diagonally
    *50 g. eggplant, sliced diagonally
    *50 g. okra, sliced diagonally
    *50 g. sitaw, sliced into 3 inches
    *50 g. kangkong stalks, sliced into 3 inches
    *50 g. kangkong leaves
    *1 (20g) pack Knorr Sinigang sa Sampalok Mix Original

  • Step 1:Begin by using the water from when you washed your rice and pour this into the pot together with the onions and tomatoes. Bring this to a simmer for about 8 minutes.

  • Step 2:Throw in the shrimp, long green chilli, radish, eggplant and okra and let this simmer until cooked through. Make sure that you have removed the black veins from the back of the shrimp with the help of a sharp pairing knife and that you have washed the head in cold running water because this is where the shrimp feces comes out.

  • Step 3:Long beans, kangkong stalks and leaves are added at the last minute before pouring in the Knorr Sinigang sa Sampalok Mix Original. Give this a gentle stir and allow to simmer for a few minutes. If you want more of that “asim kilig” goodness, try using 1 (40g) pack of Knorr Sinigang sa Sampalok Mix Original.

  • Step 4:With a bowl of patis and calamansi, and steaming hot rice, what more can your family ask for.

      Kare Kareng Hipon Recipe

    Kare Kareng Hipon Recipe
    *4 tbsp rice flour
    *2 cups water
    *2 pcs Knorr Shrimp Cubes
    *1 cup peanut butter
    *1/4 cup oil
    *1 tbsp atsuete
    *1 pc eggplant, sliced
    *5 pcs sitaw, sliced
    *1 pc banana heart, sliced
    *1 pc petchay Tagalog
    *1/2 kg shrimps
    *1/2 cup bagoong alamang

  • Step 1:Let’s begin by mixing the rice flour with water and bring this to a boil. Slowly turn the heat to a simmer.

  • Step 2:Drop in the Knorr Shrimp Cubes and peanut butter then stir gently.

  • Step 3:Get a pan nice and pour in atsuete seeds and oil. Allow to sizzle until the seeds extract their color. They should turn dark. Now, get a strainer and discard the seeds. Next, into the pot of boiling rice flour goes the atsuete oil.

  • Step 4:Gently add eggplant, sitaw, banana heart, pechay and the shrimps. Now let this simmer for 15 minutes and that’s our dish. With Kare Kareng Hipon, served with bagoong on the side, you need not feel guilty. Eat as much as you want!

      Ginataang Hipon Recipe

    Ginataang Hipon
    *1 Tbsp. oil
    *1/4 cup leeks, sliced
    *1/2 kg. shrimps, trimmed
    *1 (33g) pack Knorr Ginataang Gulay Recipe
    *1 cup water
    *1 tsp. garlic bits

  • Step 1: Get your pan nice and hot over medium heat. Pour some oil and sautè leeks until fragrant. Add shrimp next and sautè for another minute.
  • Step 2:In goes the water and Knorr Ginataang Gulay Recipe. Mix well and bring this to a boil.
  • Step 3:Simmer until the shrimps are cooked through. And that's our dish! Serve Ginataang Hipon with garlic bits on top and see how it will disappear right before your eyes. Yummy!
